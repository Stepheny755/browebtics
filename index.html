Fury

About

The St. Georges Robotics Team (SGS Robotics) is team of 20 highly motivated students from St. Georges School. Our group consists of people who are dedicated and curious about AUV and shares a passion on the field of robotics.

Meet Fury.



A re-purposed 1990's era Ikelite video camera housing modified with a replacement cast acrylic tube to increase length to 22".

• Propulsion - twin 72W brushed motors with custom kort nozzles & 60 mm three bladed propellors.
• Propulsion Configuration - differential steering with variable pitch mechanism.
• Processor core - Raspberry Pi 3.
• Sensors - OceanServer OS-5000USD w/ 24bit AD depth sensor option, MEAS pressure transducer, CMU5 pixycam robot vision.
• Power - 11.1V LiPo pack for propulsion, consumer USB cell phone battery bank for Processor core, AA battery pack for misc. auxiliary circuits.
